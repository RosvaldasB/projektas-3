Privacy statement

At Trend, we take your privacy seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information. This privacy statement outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide to us through our website and services.

Collection of Information: We may collect personal information from you when you register as a member or participate in our events and activities. This information may include your name, email address, phone number, job title, and company information.

Use of Information: We use your personal information to provide you with access to our services and to communicate with you about events and other opportunities related to our business club. We may also use your information to improve our services and to personalize your experience with us.

Safeguarding of Information: We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. We store your information in a secure environment and use industry-standard security protocols to safeguard it.

Disclosure of Information: We do not share or sell your personal information to third parties, except as required by law or as necessary to provide our services to you.

By using our website and services, you consent to our collection, use, and disclosure of your personal information as outlined in this privacy statement. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@trendbusinessclub.com.

Thank you for choosing Trend as your business club of choice. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.
